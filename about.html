Our Story

AshWing Farm has been a cornerstone of sustainable agriculture in Cork, Ireland, since 1901. As a family-run operation for four generations, we are proud of our heritage and commitment to growing organic produce that nourishes our community and the environment.

Our farm practices reflect our values of sustainability, tradition, and innovation, ensuring that future generations can enjoy the land as much as we do today.
